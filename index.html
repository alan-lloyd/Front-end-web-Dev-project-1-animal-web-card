Dolphin

Dolphins are mammals that have interacted with humans, and also some ocean-based species, since ancient times. There are recorded cases where they saved people (and whales and dogs) from drowning or from attack by sharks

Scientific name: Parvorder Odontoceti
Average length: 6-30 feet
Average lifespan: 46 years (female); 31 year (male)
Habitat: They generally prefer warmer water

Dolphins are found throughout the world, in both oceans and major rivers (e.g Amazon and Indus).
